Hiring Essentials

The nice thing about beginning hiking is that you don't really need any special gear, you can probably get away with things you already have. Let's start with clothing. A typical mistake hiking beginners makeis wearing jeans and regular clothes, which will get heavy and chafe if they get sweaty or wet.
